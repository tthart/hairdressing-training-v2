    About

    Thank you for using the Hairdressing Training app.

    The app has been developed to allow teachers; students; trainers or anyone with an interest in hairdressing and barbering skills access to step-by-step training videos on the move - all mapped to the NVQ curriculum. The video content has been produced by FE colleges to ensure accuracy.

    The app is funded by Jisc, and developed by Mimas taking content from Mimas' award winning service - Hairdressing Training.
